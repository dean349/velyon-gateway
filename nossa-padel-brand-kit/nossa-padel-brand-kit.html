01Brand Foundation

Who We Are

Mission

"We stripped away the noise. Durable materials, balanced design, and a racket that feels right from day one."

NOSSA was founded on the belief that the padel market was overcrowded with confusion. We exist to make premium padel equipment accessible, beautiful, and honest — built around community, not hype.

Name Meaning

"In Portuguese, NOSSA means ours."

Padel is a game built for four, where anyone can join, everyone belongs, and new friendships are made every week. The name anchors the brand in community, inclusivity, and shared experience.

Positioning

Premium without pretension

High-end equipment at an honest price. Quality-led, community-first.

Heritage

Portuguese roots

Inspired by Iberian culture — warmth, community, passion for sport.

Promise

Feel right from day one

Months of R&D, 12K carbon, feel-first design. No gimmicks. Just quality.

Logo Anatomy

  • The MarkAn abstract figure in motion — three crescent-arc forms suggesting a padel player mid-swing, with a circular form at the apex representing the ball. Conveys kinetics, precision, and humanity.
  • The Wordmark"NOSSA" set in a bold, geometric sans-serif with custom letterforms. The triangular 'A' (no crossbar) reinforces the angular, modern aesthetic. Always uppercase.
  • CompositionMark leads left, wordmark follows right. Clear-space equal to the height of the 'N' on all sides. Never rearrange.
  • ColourwaysBlack on white. White on black. Single-colour only — no gradients, outlines, or drop shadows on the logo.
✓
Do: Maintain clear spaceAlways preserve a minimum clear zone equal to the cap-height of 'N' around the full logo.
✓
Do: Use approved colourways onlyBlack on white/light, or white on black/dark. No colour logos.
✗
Don't: Stretch, rotate, or recolourNever alter proportions, add effects, use product accent colours, or place on busy backgrounds.

06Photography

Imagery Direction

Style 1 — Cinematic Dark Studio
Style 2 — Neutral Studio
Style 3 — Product Hero
Style 4 — Bundle / E-commerce
Style 5 — Lifestyle / On Court
01
Cinematic Dark Studio
Products photographed against near-black backgrounds with dramatic controlled lighting. Reveals texture, carbon weave, and material quality. High contrast. No props. Used for hero and editorial contexts.
02
Neutral Studio
Clean off-white or light grey studio. Product-centred compositions. Used for product listings, PDPs, and bundles. Square or portrait format. No distractions — let the design speak.
03
Lifestyle / On Court
Natural outdoor court settings. Player in motion or at rest. Authentic — not staged. Documentary feel. Used for social media, brand pages, and community content. Green court tones complement brand accent palette.

Homepage hero copy
✓ Do Write Like This
  • Short, punchy sentences. Impact first.
  • Use "we" and "you" — speak human to human
  • Lead with the benefit, back with the proof
  • Use specific details: "12K carbon", "months of R&D"
  • Community is a genuine word for us — use it
  • Reference Portuguese heritage when authentic
✗ Never Write Like This
  • "Revolutionising the game" — empty claim
  • Jargon overload aimed at confusing players
  • Passive voice — "this racket has been designed to..."
  • Price-led messaging as the primary value prop
  • Over-promising performance claims you can't back
  • Formal corporate language — we're a community brand
